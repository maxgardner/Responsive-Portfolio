About Me

Hello! My name is Max Gardner, and I currently work in the tech industry as a Merchant Success Representative at Cratejoy. I graduated summa cum laude from the Honors College at the University of Houston (2014) with dual degrees in journalism and creative writing.

My passions are technology, communications, and media, and I am growing increasingly interested in the way we can use technology to affect communication and shape it moving forward. I am a proficient creative, academic, and editorial writer and editor with experience in graphic and web design.

I am currently enrolled in a six-month program with the Coding Bootcamp at UT Austin to earn a full-stack web development certificate.
